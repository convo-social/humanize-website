Convo Study Privacy Policy

What You Need to Know (Summary)

If you're about to participate in a Convo AI-powered research focus group or interview (each, a "Study"), here's what you need to know:• Your Study is moderated by an AI, not a human. The moderator you interact with ("Peggy") is an artificial intelligence system. There is no human moderator present during the session.
‍
• Your session will be recorded. We collect your audio and/or video responses, which are transcribed and analyzed using AI technology.
• Your Study may be a group session. If so, other participants will hear what you say. We cannot guarantee that other participants will keep the discussion confidential.
• A research client is receiving your insights. The Study may be commissioned by a Convo customer ("Research Client"). If so, your responses — including transcripts and AI-generated analysis — will be shared with that Research Client.
• Research Clients must follow our Acceptable Use Policy and may only use your responses for authorized research purposes.
• Your personal data is protected by industry-standard security measures.
• You can exercise your privacy rights at any time. If you'd like to access, correct, or delete your data, or if you have any questions, please contact us at orr@useconvo.ai.See the full Study Privacy Policy below for details.

"Personal Data" means any information that identifies or relates to a particular individual.This Policy applies only to your participation in a Study. For information about how we handle data from website visitors and customers, please see our Website Privacy Policy.

Personal Data We CollectWhen you participate in a Study, we may collect:2.1 Screening DataInformation you provide during the screening and recruitment process, such as your name, email address, demographic information, and responses to qualifying questions. This data helps us determine your eligibility for a Study.2.2 Session DataAudio and/or video recordings, transcripts, and any responses you provide during the Study. These may include Personal Data you choose to share during the conversation.2.3 Technical DataIP address, device information, browser settings, and connection data to ensure a stable and secure session experience.2.4 Scheduling & Contact DataName, email address, phone number, and scheduling preferences used to coordinate your participation.3. How We Use Your Personal DataWe collect and use your Personal Data for the following purposes:• Conduct the Study: Record, transcribe, process, and analyze your responses to generate research insights for the Research Client who commissioned the Study.
• AI-Powered Analysis: Use artificial intelligence to transcribe your responses, identify themes and patterns, and generate structured research insights. This analysis is performed automatically and may involve multiple stages of AI processing.
• Manage Your Participation: Schedule sessions, send reminders, process compensation, and communicate with you about the Study.
• Service Improvement: Use aggregated, de-identified, or anonymized data to improve our platform's functionality, AI moderation capabilities, and overall research quality.
• Legal Compliance: Comply with applicable laws, regulations, and legal obligations.4. How We Share Your Personal DataWe do not sell your Personal Data to third parties or use it for advertising or targeted marketing.We share your Personal Data only with:• The Research Client: The organization that commissioned the Study. Research Clients receive your responses, transcripts, AI-generated analysis, and insights. Research Clients are contractually required to use your data only for authorized research purposes and to implement appropriate safeguards to protect your Personal Data.
• Service Providers: Third-party companies that help us deliver the Services, such as cloud hosting, audio/video processing, and transcription services. These providers process your data only on our behalf and under our instructions.
• Other Participants: If the Study is conducted in a group format, other participants in the session will hear your responses in real time. See Section 6 (Group Session Disclosure) below.
• Legal Authorities: If required by law, court order, or to protect the rights, property, or safety of Convo, our users, or others.5. AI Processing DisclosureYour Study is moderated by an artificial intelligence system, not a human moderator. The AI moderator ("Peggy") facilitates the conversation, asks questions, and guides the discussion.Your responses undergo the following AI processing:1. Real-time moderation — the AI moderator processes your responses during the session to guide the conversation.
2. Transcription — your audio/video is transcribed into text using AI-powered speech recognition.
3. Analysis — AI systems analyze transcripts to extract themes, patterns, emotional signals, and research insights.
4. Insight delivery — structured analysis is delivered to the Research Client through our platform.No fully automated decisions with legal or similarly significant effects are made about you based on your Study responses. The AI processing is used solely to generate qualitative research insights.By participating in the Study, you consent to this AI-mediated interaction and processing.6. Group Session DisclosureStudies may be conducted in a group format ("focus group") with multiple participants present simultaneously.If you are in a group session:• Other participants will hear your responses in real time during the session.
• Convo cannot guarantee confidentiality of what is discussed, as we cannot control what other participants do with information they hear during the session.
• Do not share information that you would not want other participants to hear.
• Transcripts and analysis shared with the Research Client will include responses from all participants in the session.Your participation in a group session constitutes your acknowledgment and acceptance of these limitations.7. Regardless of how you were recruited:• This Policy governs how Convo handles your Personal Data during and after the Study.
• Consent provided to a recruitment platform does not substitute for the consent you provide to Convo. By joining a Study, you consent to the data collection and processing described in this Policy.
• The recruitment platform's own terms and privacy policies continue to apply to your relationship with that platform separately.8. Your Rights and ChoicesDepending on your location and applicable law (e.g., GDPR, CCPA/CPRA), you may have rights including:• Access: Request access to the Personal Data we hold about you.
• Correction: Update or correct inaccuracies in your Personal Data.
• Deletion: Request deletion of your Personal Data where legally permitted.
• Objection/Restriction: Object to or restrict certain data processing activities.
• Data Portability: Receive a copy of your Personal Data in a structured, commonly used format.
• Withdraw Consent: You may withdraw your consent to data processing at any time. Withdrawal does not affect the lawfulness of processing that occurred before withdrawal.
• Non-Discrimination: We will not discriminate against you for exercising your privacy rights.To exercise these rights, contact orr@useconvo.ai. We will respond within the timeframe required by applicable law.Note: If you withdraw consent or request deletion before a Study is complete, we may not be able to include your responses in the research deliverable, and this may affect your eligibility for compensation.10.
